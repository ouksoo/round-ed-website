introduction
rounded

Rounded is a group consisted of creative design and interactive coding directors.
With surplus experience in design and coding, members of Rounded identify and
reject any risks of a given project before-hand and carry each and every project to
success.

With a trusting bond between Rounded and major advertising agencies as a base,
Rounded makes better results through correct design consulting and thorough
knowledge of the latest technologies.

The abundant experience and instinct of the directors of Rounded will be the priority
of choosing Rounded as a accompnaying partner. The balance of creative design and
technology will be a stable clockwork.

story of
rounded

Growing in a unique and distinct galaxy, each and every director of Rounded experienced and
acquired specific skill sets with instinct and intuition in their fields of work.
Directors from each planet with unique skill sets started combining their skills equally to produce a
new form of results never before seen in history.
These attempts with unlimited possibilities lead the directors to make a group called Rounded
in planet Earth to stabalize their experiments.
How the diverse experiments of Rounded will lead to powerful synergy effect and experiences to
each directors is unpredictable to us also.
